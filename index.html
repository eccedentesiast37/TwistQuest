📜 How to Play

  1. There are 3 levels — Easy, Medium, and Hard.
  2. You get 3 lives (❤️) per level.
  3. A tongue twister will be displayed. Press Start to begin.
  4. Speak the tongue twister clearly into your microphone.
  5. Our AI evaluates your accuracy (CER) and speed (WPM).
  6. Meet the threshold → advance! Fail 3 times → Game Over.
